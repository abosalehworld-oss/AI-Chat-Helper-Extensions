🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

🛡️ Zero Data Collection

Overview

AI Chat Helper is a Chrome extension that helps you copy and summarize your AI chat conversations. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed with a privacy-first architecture.

TL;DR: We collect ZERO data. Everything happens locally on your device. No data is ever sent to any server, period.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any data. Specifically:

  • ❌ No personal information is collected
  • ❌ No browsing history is tracked
  • ❌ No conversation content is stored or transmitted
  • ❌ No analytics or telemetry data is gathered
  • ❌ No cookies are read or written
  • ❌ No third-party services are contacted
  • ❌ No advertising or marketing data is collected

How the Extension Works

When you click the extension icon on an AI chat page:

  • The extension reads the visible conversation text from the current page DOM
  • All text processing (formatting, summarization) happens entirely in your browser
  • Copied text goes to your local clipboard only
  • No data is sent to any external server or API

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Allows the extension to read the content of the currently active tab, but ONLY when you click the extension icon. No background access.
  • storage — Used to save your preferences (like UI settings) locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API.
  • scripting — Allows the extension to inject the content extraction script into the active tab when you request a copy or summary.

Summarization

Text summarization is performed using:

  • A local extractive algorithm that runs entirely in your browser
  • Optionally, Chrome's built-in AI (Gemini Nano) if available — this also runs on your device

No text is ever sent to external AI services for summarization.

Data Storage

The only data stored locally is:

  • Your UI preferences (e.g., preferred copy format, summary length)
  • This data is stored using chrome.storage.local and never leaves your device

Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any third-party services, APIs, or servers. It is a fully offline, self-contained tool.

Updates to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last Updated" date. The core principle — zero data collection — will never change.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please file an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
